Things to do

Around our SeaView Apartment, there are a lot of things to do and see. We have created a list of the top places and activities to do while you stay with us. It contains a variety of different things that will suit everyone!

Back to top

Joys

This is Perranporth's smallest shop, but don't be fooled! This small space is overflowing with beautifully handcrafted jewellery, Oracle cards, gorgeous crystals and beautiful meditation CD's.

Higgins Gift Shop

Full of every trinket or gift you can think of, Higgins Gift Shop supplies everything from kids wetsuits to locally crafted jewellery.

Perranporth Beach

It's the beach everyone raves about and billed as a great place for surfing, snorkelling, sailing and generally splashing around. Because the beach is so huge, it almost feels as if you have the place to yourself and the fresh air off the ocean is exhilarating. Early morning you'll probably see a few horses and their riders having a paddle in the surf and there's a welcome cafe for a coffee stop halfway along.

Perranporth Museum

This Museum offers an insight into the social and industrial past of Perranporth and the surrounding villages, with displays of mining, fishing, costumes, farming, surfing, a replica of a Cornish kitchen and much more.

Chrissy's Tearoom

High Tea is served here from 3pm daily or, if you fancy a delicious home made cake, fresh sandwich or fine cup of tea, Chrissy's could be the perfect stop off for you. All ingredients are locally sourced.

Perran Dairy Ice Cream Parlour

Perran Dairy Ice Cream achieved five out of five bubbles and 122 views on the go-to travel website. As a new entry for 2015, the venue is located just a short walk from Perranporth Beach and serves up homemade artisan ice cream made from local clotted cream and milk. Visitors can also enjoy treats such as Belgium waffles, sundaes and crepes.

Willow Bistro

Willow is a family run licensed Cafe/Bistro, They serve Breakfast, Lunch & Evening meals. Their current menu covers meat, fish, pasta and also light meals, also they have a good selection of vegetarian dishes available.

The Watering Hole

Beach bar/restaurant with a simple menu of burgers, snacks and pies, sea view terrace and TV sport.

South West Coast Path - Cligga Head

Take a short stroll through the silent remnants of what was once one of Cornwall's busiest mining areas. In summer this is a riot of colour, with blazing gorse bushes and banks of vivid heather, wildflowers dotted between them and the mineral-stained cliffs startlingly red against the blue sea. In places the stony path passes close to high cliffs.

Ticket to Ride Surf School

Perranporth is a growing seaside town with a stunning beach. With onsite parking, changing room facilities, a surf shop and small surf groups - you're all set for the best surf lesson experience from start to finish. You'll see your surfing improve in no time at all with us, as we always aim to tailor our surf lessons to the group's specific surfing needs.
