Introduction

Looking for a perfect trip to the coast with your friends? Don't have much money but still want a good quality place to stay? Want a place that is close to everything you need? You have come to the right place then as SeaView Apartment will fulfil your needs!

The SeaView Apartment is situated in North-west of Cornwall, with only a 15-minute walk to Perranporth beach, one of the most popular beaches in the whole of England. Not only that, that is only one of the 42 beaches in North Cornish Coast which means you will have plenty of choices on where to go and our apartment will provide easy access to all of them.

Our website contains all the information you need such as a description of the facilities in the apartment, places of interest nearby, directions and we have an electronic visitor book where you can read comments from our previous happy visitors. We aim to provide the best experience for everyone however if you do have any questions or issues, please contact us about it and we will try to reply as soon as possible.
